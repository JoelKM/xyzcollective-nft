LORE

TEN YEARS AGO, THE COUNTDOWN STARTED.

After thousands years of destruction, humanity’s home was finally reaching it’s last days. The atmosphere was slowly collapsing in on itself, in turn creating a mixture of poisonous gases that could wipe out the entire population within hours of exposure.

The only way to survive was to adapt.


AT FIRST IT, WAS JUST AN IDEA.

Creating an exo-suit that somehow filtered the poisonous air well enough for human consumption seemed farfetched as a plan, but they didn’t have many ideas left.

In the end, thousands were produced only a year before the apocalypse.


BY THAT TIME.

By that time, the hints of Earth’s demise were starting to show. Many cities were underwater, and the sky had gained a permanent purple hue. Crops stopped growing and only genetically modified plants were able to thrive, - if only for a short time.


THE FACTORY.

The Factory was working on producing as many suits as possible with the limited manpower and resources they had. It was a hurried process, perhaps rushed as well, which later would lead to what people called “The Beginning of the End.”
